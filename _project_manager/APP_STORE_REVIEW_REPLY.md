## Guideline 2.1(b) – In-App Purchases (Steps to locate IAPs)

Our In-App Purchases fall into two groups: consumable Energy packs (Starter Kick, First Purchase, Value Pack, Power User, Ultimate Saver, etc.) and the Energy Pass subscription (weekly, monthly, yearly). Both are available as follows.

**Consumable Energy packs (Energy Store)**

1. Open the app and complete or dismiss the initial onboarding screen (e.g. the “You’re All Set!” step).

2. On the main Home screen (the tab that shows the food timeline), locate the Energy indicator at the top of the screen. It appears as a pill-shaped element showing the current Energy balance (e.g. “10” or “50” with a lightning icon).

3. Tap this Energy indicator once. The Energy Store screen will open.

4. On the Energy Store screen, all consumable In-App Purchase offers are listed: Starter Kick (100 Energy), First Purchase (200 Energy), Value Pack (550 Energy), Power User (1,200 Energy), Ultimate Saver (2,000 Energy), and any other active offers or promotions.

**Energy Pass (subscription)**

The Energy Pass subscription can be reached in either of two ways:

- **From the Energy Store:** After opening the Energy Store as above, scroll down. A section or button labelled “Energy Pass” is shown (for users who are not already subscribers). Tapping it opens the subscription screen where the weekly, monthly, and yearly Energy Pass options are available.

- **From Profile:** Open the Profile tab (the rightmost tab in the bottom navigation). In the Profile screen, tap the row or card labelled “Energy Pass”. The subscription screen opens, where the weekly, monthly, and yearly Energy Pass plans can be viewed and purchased.

All In-App Purchases are set up for the Apple Sandbox and function with Sandbox tester accounts. We do not apply any storefront or device-based restrictions that would prevent reviewers from seeing these offers. Some consumable offers may only appear for first-time purchasers; the main Energy packs and the Energy Pass subscription are visible as described above.
