Privacy Policy

MiRO — My Intake Record Oracle

Effective Date: February 17, 2026
Last Updated: February 17, 2026

Your food data stays on your device. No account required. No personal data collected. Energy balance synced securely via Firebase.

1. Information We Collect

1.1 Data Stored Locally on Your Device (Offline-First)

The following data is stored only on your device and is never transmitted to our servers:

  • Food entries (name, calories, nutrients, photos)
  • Health goals (daily calorie goal, macro targets, water goal)
  • Basic personal information (name, gender, age, weight, height, activity level)
  • Cuisine preference (your typical cuisine selection)
  • Created meal recipes (My Meals) and saved ingredients
  • AI Chat history and chat sessions
  • App settings and preferences

1.2 Data Transmitted to Our Servers

  • Device ID — A unique identifier generated for your device, used to manage Energy balance and prevent fraud. This is NOT linked to your personal identity.
  • Energy balance — Synced with Firebase Firestore for persistence across app reinstalls.
  • Food photos (temporarily) — When you use AI analysis, your food photo is sent to our backend server (Firebase Cloud Functions) for processing by Google Gemini AI. Photos are processed in real-time and are not stored on our servers.
  • Chat messages (temporarily) — When using MiRO AI chat, your messages are sent to our backend for AI processing. Messages are processed in real-time and are not stored on our servers.
  • Referral codes — If you use the referral system, your MiRO ID and referral interactions are stored in Firebase.

1.3 Data We Do NOT Collect

  • No email address
  • No phone number
  • No real name (unless you optionally set one in your profile)
  • No location data
  • No contacts or address book
  • No advertising identifiers

2. Third-Party Services

2.1 Google Play Billing

  • Energy Token purchases and Energy Pass subscriptions are processed via Google Play Billing
  • Payment information is handled entirely by Google — we never see your credit card or payment details
  • We only store the Product ID and Purchase Token to verify purchases

2.2 Firebase Services (Google)

  • Cloud Functions — Processes AI analysis requests and manages Energy deduction
  • Firestore — Stores Energy balance, subscription status, referral data, and gamification state
  • Firebase Analytics — Collects anonymous usage statistics (e.g., which features are used most, error rates). No personal data is included. You can opt out via your device's analytics settings.

2.3 Google Gemini AI

  • Food photos and text descriptions are processed by Google Gemini AI via our Firebase backend
  • Data is processed in real-time and not stored by our backend
  • Google's AI processing is subject to Google's Privacy Policy

3. Gallery Access

  • MiRO can scan your photo gallery for food photos (Pull-to-Refresh feature)
  • Gallery scanning is performed entirely on your device using ML Kit (on-device machine learning)
  • No gallery photos are uploaded to our servers unless you explicitly choose to analyze a photo with AI
  • You can control gallery scan settings (date range, image limit) in Profile → Photo Scan Settings

4. Subscription Data

  • If you subscribe to Energy Pass, your subscription status is stored in Firebase Firestore (linked to your Device ID)
  • Subscription management (renewal, cancellation) is handled through Google Play
  • We store: subscription status, start date, expiry date, and product ID

5. Referral System

  • Your MiRO ID (a unique code) may be shared voluntarily with friends for referral rewards
  • Referral interactions (who referred whom) are stored in Firebase Firestore
  • No personal information is shared between referrer and referee — only MiRO IDs

6. Required Permissions

Permission Purpose Required?
Camera Take food photos for AI analysis Optional (needed for photo analysis)
Photos/Gallery Select food photos from gallery; auto-scan for food photos Optional (needed for gallery features)
Internet Send photos to AI backend; sync Energy balance; process purchases Required for AI features; not needed for manual logging

7. Data Storage and Security

  • All food data and personal information stored locally on your device
  • Energy balance and subscription data synchronized with Firebase Firestore (encrypted in transit)
  • Sensitive data (Energy tokens) stored using Flutter Secure Storage (encrypted at rest)
  • Energy transactions secured via Firebase with device authentication
  • Payment processing handled securely by Google Play Billing

8. Backup and Data Portability

  • You can export your food data and Energy balance using the Backup feature (Profile → Backup Data)
  • Backup files are saved locally on your device — we do not have access to them
  • You can import data from a backup file (Profile → Restore from Backup)
  • Transfer Keys are one-time use and expire after 30 days

9. User Rights

  • Delete all local data anytime (Profile → Clear All Data)
  • Uninstall the app to remove all local data
  • Energy balance persists across app reinstalls (linked to your device)
  • Request server-side data deletion (Energy balance, referral data) by contacting support
  • Export your data at any time using the Backup feature

10. Children's Privacy

MiRO is not intended for children under 13 years of age. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children.

11. Data Retention

  • Local food data: Retained until you delete it or uninstall the app
  • Energy balance: Retained indefinitely (linked to your device)
  • Subscription data: Retained while subscription is active, plus as required by law
  • Referral data: Retained indefinitely
  • Purchase records: Retained as required by Google Play and tax regulations
  • Analytics data: Subject to Google's data retention policies

12. International Users

MiRO is available globally. Data processing occurs on Firebase servers (Google Cloud). By using MiRO, you consent to the processing of your data as described in this policy, regardless of your location.

13. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Changes will be communicated through app updates. Continued use of the app after changes constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.

14. Contact Us

If you have questions about this Privacy Policy, please contact us through Google Play Store or via the app's support channel.
